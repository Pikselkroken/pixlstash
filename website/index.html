What it is

A smarter home for your images

PixlStash is a self-hosted image management platform that automatically imports, tags and indexes your entire library.

Search, tag, drop the rubbish, and keep your best work. Access across your devices. Right from your browser!

Built for

Anyone drowning in images

Whether you're a photographer culling shoots, an AI trainer curating datasets or a hobbyist with a large image collection, PixlStash helps you bring order to the chaos.

Handle tens of thousands of images with ease, and spend more time creating and less time organizing.
